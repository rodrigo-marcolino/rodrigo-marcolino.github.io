My Learning Plan

By Rodrigo Marcolino | Posted on: 5 July 2021

What is my long term goal and/or Career Pathway?

My long-term goal to become a successful and proficient T-Shaped Developer.

A description of your own strengths and limitations in relation to learning

I can say that my main strength is that I do love the process of learning.

My biggest limitation is that I always find myself trying to learn about too many things at once. consequently, I tend to not spend enough time practicing the fundamentals of a specific subject.

What skills (non-technical - human skills) would you like to see developed in yourself while at EDA?

Due to my lack of English proficiency. My communication skills have been drastically reduced, and I see here at EDA a great opportunity to improve my communication skills.

Workload Management:

- EDA has provided an excellent study plan, and I'll stick to it.
- I'll Divide the work into chunks that have a beginning and an end.
- Keep my focus on a whole section or topic each time.
- I will utilise Toggle to keep track of time spent on activities. with 25 minutes spent working followed by a five-minute break.

A commitment as to how and where you will seek help in a timely way

In Case that I'm feeling like I'm not making any progress, I'll ask for help. EDA has provided a slack Channel that allows us to get the support we need.
